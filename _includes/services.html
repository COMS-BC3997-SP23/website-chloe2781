Purpose

nailARt is an app that leverages AR (Augmented Technology) to provide a more accurate and convenient way of selecting nail designs.
This app will allow users to preview different designs on their own hands in real-time through the use of their phone camera.
In short, this app is a social media platform for nail art viewing, creating, and sharing (think about Instagram and Snapchat combined!).

Save Time

Having your nails done take a lot of time, and it's even worse if you don't even like the results. Let NailARt save some time for you.

Save Money

Looking at a $100 set of nails you don't like is torture. Let NailARt save you a lot of money.

Satisfaction (finally)

What's better than seeing the nail ideas you've been thinking of in your head on your hand before spending $100 on them? NailARt.
